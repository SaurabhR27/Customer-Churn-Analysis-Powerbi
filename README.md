# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical business challenges faced by subscription-based companies. Acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones. This project analyzes customer behavior patterns to identify factors contributing to churn and provides actionable insights for improving customer retention.

This dashboard was developed using Power BI and focuses on customer demographics, subscription behavior, spending patterns, customer support interactions, and revenue impact.

---

## Business Problem

The organization is experiencing customer attrition and wants to understand:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- What factors influence customer churn?
- Which subscription plans generate the most revenue loss?
- How can customer retention be improved?

## Key Insights

### Insight 1
Customers with higher payment delays show significantly higher churn rates.

### Insight 2
Customers with frequent support interactions are more likely to leave.

### Insight 3
Short-tenure customers represent the highest churn segment.

### Insight 4
Certain subscription plans contribute disproportionately to revenue loss.

### Insight 5
Higher usage frequency is associated with stronger customer retention.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Implement proactive retention campaigns for new customers.
2. Improve payment reminder systems to reduce payment delays.
3. Enhance customer support quality and response times.
4. Offer incentives for long-term contract adoption.
5. Monitor high-risk customer segments using churn indicators.
